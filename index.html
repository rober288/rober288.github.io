The Cat You Need To Meet

The Best Cat Ever

Trust Me...

Fluffernog the whiskerblitz cat, all sproingy and marmalade-slick, tiptoed over the crinklemat with eyes like moonbean saucers. With a snerk and a blink, it nommed the cork of the vintage snoozleberry wine bottle, chomping with the tenacity of a jellybean tornado. “Meowf!” it yodeled, tongue all glass-sparkly and paws flailing in a ballet of nonsense. The bottle gave a shloop and a glorp as the cat’s molars turned to rubber duckies mid-crunch. Purple fizzlejuice burbled up and poofed into sparklemist. A passing toaster goose honked thrice, blessing the wine feast with its crumbly squawk. Meanwhile, the cat levitated slightly, fueled by fermented dreamjuice and chaos fluff. Its tail scribbled the alphabet backward into the air while a nearby carrot applauded. As reality melted like butter on a trampoline, Fluffernog belched a tiny jazz band and curled up in a puddle of temporal mewsic. All was squibble.
